What is ToolKit Pro?

ToolKit Pro is a free online tools platform offering 15+ essential calculators, converters, and productivity tools for students, developers, professionals, and everyday users. Whether you need to convert metres to feet, generate a secure QR code, calculate your loan EMI, run scientific computations, or check your BMI — ToolKit Pro delivers instant, accurate results with zero sign-up and no hidden costs.

Every tool runs entirely inside your browser, meaning your data never leaves your device. ToolKit Pro is built for speed, accuracy, and simplicity — no clutter, no unnecessary steps, just results.

  • Unit Converter — Convert length, weight, temperature, speed, area and digital storage across 6 categories with live swap.
  • Currency Converter — Reference rates for 9 major world currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, PKR, AED, INR and more.
  • Scientific Calculator — Full-featured with trigonometry, logarithms, powers, memory functions and DEG/RAD modes.
  • QR Code Generator — Generate scannable QR codes for URLs, text, email addresses, and WiFi networks. Download as PNG.
  • Loan EMI Calculator — Calculate monthly EMI, total interest, and view a full amortization schedule for any loan.
  • BMI Calculator — Body Mass Index with WHO category, animated scale, metric and imperial support.
  • GPA Calculator — Add multiple subjects with credit hours and grades to compute cumulative GPA on a 4.0 scale.
  • Password Generator — Create strong, random passwords with customizable length and character sets.
